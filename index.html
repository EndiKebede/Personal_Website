Environmental data & sustainability science for resilient food–water systems

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Geography at the University of Delaware (expected May 2026), working on global irrigation, water scarcity, and food sustainability in the 21st century.

My research develops and leverages large-scale environmental datasets, remote sensing, and geospatial analytics to quantify risks, trade-offs, and opportunities across the water–food–energy nexus.
